French Toast

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • ⅔ cup milk
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 8 slices of bread
  • butter

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk and cinnamon. Whisk the mixture until well blended and pour into a shallow bowl, wide enough to place the slice of bread you will be using.
  2. Place each slice of bread into the milk egg mixture, allowing the bread to soak in some of it.
