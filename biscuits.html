Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour.
  • 1 tbs baking powder
  • 1 tbs granulated sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ⅓ cup butter (cold)
  • 1 cup milk or buttermilk
  • 2 tbs butter melted (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Use an ungreased baking sheet or line it with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, stir together 2 cups flour, 1 tbs baking powder, 1 tbs granulated sugar, 1 tbs salt.
  3. Grate ⅓ cup butter (cold).and stir into the flour mixture.
  4. Gradually pour in 1 cup milk or buttermilk while stirring until dough just comes together. Avoid overmixing to keep the biscuits tender.
  5. Turn the dough onto a clean, lightly floured countertop. Gather it into a ball and press down until it is about 1 inch thick.
