Pancakes

Ingredients Measurements
Flour 1½ cups all-purpose
Baking powder 3½ tsp
Sugar 1 tbs
Salt ¼ tsp
Milk 1¼ cups
Melted butter 3 tbs
Eggs 1 large

Directions

  1. Yields 8 servings.
  2. Gather all ingredients.
  3. Sift flour, baking powder,sugar and salt together in a large bowl. Make a well in the center and add milk, melted butter and egg; mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop approximately ¼ cup of the batter onto the pan for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry about 2 or 3 minutes.
  5. Flip and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
  6. Serve and Enjoy!!!
