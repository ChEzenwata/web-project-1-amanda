Pancakes

Ingredients

  • Yields 8 servings.
  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour.
  • 3½ tsp baking powder.
  • 1 tbs white sugar.
  • ¼ tsp salt.
  • 1¼ cups milk.
  • 3 tbs butter, melted.
  • 1 large egg.

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Sift flour, baking powder,sugar and salt together in a large bowl. Make a well in the center and add milk, melted butter and egg; mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop approximately ¼ cup of the batter onto the pan for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry about 2 or 3 minutes.
  4. Flip and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
  5. Serve and Enjoy!!!
